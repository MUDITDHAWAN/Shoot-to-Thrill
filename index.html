Features

XR

Shoot to Thrill combines all real and virtual environments to give you a seamless gaming experience.

Infrared Technology

Infrared rays, emitted from the guns when triggered, are detected by sensors placed on the jacket to register when a player is hit.

Vibration Motors

The jackets are fitted with vibration motors for instantaneous feedback of a shot.

Multiplayer

Shoot to Thrill can also be played in multiplayer mode for more elaborate game plays with many people.

Score, Health Update

Get real time feedback of your ammo, as well as health of both you and your opponents.

Play Anywhere

With Shoot to Thrill, all you need to do is put your phone into a VR headset, and you are ready to start gaming wherever and whenever you want.
